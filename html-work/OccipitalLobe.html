Occipital Lobe

As the name may have suggested for some, this lobe is tasked with object recognition, color perception, depth perception etc.
The functions of this brain seem more tangible to us as it is crucial for eyesight which is in use for most of us almost constantly.
This lobe is located around the back of the brain, which would mean any injuries to that part of the brain could lead to impairment in eyesight
