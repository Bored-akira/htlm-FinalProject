Frontal Lobe

The frontal lobe is an extremely essential part of your brain. One of the important aspects is it's control of movement through the motor cortex.
The motor cortex consists of something called the brocas area which actually allows you to talk.
Another aspect of the frontal lobe is it enabling you to have self control. Alcohol is known to weaken the frontal lobe hence reducing self control giving you a pleasurable and confident feeling.
